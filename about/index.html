About

Andreas Dzialocha (*1988) is an electric bass player, composer and developer. His work consists of both digital and physical environments, spaces or platforms for performers and listeners. The computer itself serves as a customised tool, not only as an artistic device but also as a political, social or philosophical medium, questioning frameworks or dealing with machine learning and decentralized networks. He is founder of the berlin-based ensemble Serenus Zeitblom Oktett, co-publisher of the self-curated magazine platform BLATT 3000, co-founder of the label Hyperdelia and the intermedial score platform Y-E-S. He studied art history, musicology, media philosophy and computer science in Berlin where he also sometimes lives and works.
